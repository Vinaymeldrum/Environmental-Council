About the Environmental Council

What are we about?

We are going to be the change in AY. Our goal is to make AYJ as eco-friendly as possible. And to do so, we need YOU! By joining this group YOU will have the opportunity to make your ideas heard an implemented into our school. YOU will have the opportunity to change our school for the better. You will help have fundraisers to save money for non-profits organizations. And just, so much more.
