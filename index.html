Join Us

Our goal is to make A.Y. Jackson as environmentally friendly as possible, and to do so, we need your help! By joining the Environmental Council, you will have the opportunity to make your ideas heard and implemented to change our school for the better!

Not only will you help create impact within the community, but you will also be able to obtain volunteer hours, as well as be able to include this club on any resume or application!

Come join us every Friday at lunch! We meet in room 131, located within the science hall. Alternatively, email us for more details.
